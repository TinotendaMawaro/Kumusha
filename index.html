OUR STORY

THE KUMUSHA STORY

Founded by Lonestar and Ruvimbo Makoni

Our Founding Vision

Kumusha Soccer Academy was founded by Lonestar and Ruvimbo Makoni with a vision to provide elite soccer training to Zimbabwean youth. Their passion for the sport and commitment to community development led to the creation of this academy, which now stands as a beacon of hope for aspiring young athletes.

Lonestar and Ruvimbo are also the proud owners of Breaking Barriers Disability Services and Valt Hair & Beauty Salon. Through these ventures, they have built a legacy of empowerment and excellence in the community, using the academy as a platform to nurture talent and transform lives through sport.

Breaking Barriers & Valt

Breaking Barriers Disability Services, owned by Lonestar and Ruvimbo Makoni, provides essential support and inclusion for differently-abled members of our community. Valt Hair & Beauty Salon is their flagship enterprise that funds many academy initiatives. Together, these businesses embody the same spirit of resilience and creativity that drives Kumusha Soccer Academy.
